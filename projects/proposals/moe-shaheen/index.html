Make.com connects directly to WhatsApp Business API through your Google account. No extra cost beyond the Make.com subscription. Messages go out automatically from your business number, so leads see a familiar Dubai contact, not a random bot.

Non-responders get added to Mailchimp with event invites. Your business model already handles this: they attend a wrestling event, get inspired, and sign up later. The system keeps the connection alive instead of letting them disappear.
