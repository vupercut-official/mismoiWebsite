# Module 7: Content Strategy

> "Social proof trumps CTAs, content strategy, and sales tactics."

Content is not about going viral. It's not about vanity metrics. Content is a conversion tool. Every post, every story, every highlight exists to build authority and make the DM outreach easier. This module covers Max's Selective Exposure Funnel and how to engineer content for sales.

---

## Selective Exposure Funnel

Max's signature methodology and the thing that makes him unique. This is what he teaches all his clients.

Key elements:
- Strategic exposure of social proof over time
- Not about going viral. About targeted authority building.
- Content engineered for sales, not vanity metrics
- Constant social proof creates perceived uniqueness and superiority

---

## Social Proof Loops

### The Inevitability Bridge

- Show every possible ICP problem solved with your clients
- Talk about transformation from problem to solution
- People want to work with you and don't even know why
- They see connection + transformation + social proof

### Continuous Reinforcement

- Story highlights that continuously reinforce credibility
- Address common objections through sheer volume
- Every objection destroyed by the volume of client results

### Social Proof Over Everything

- Social proof trumps CTAs, content strategy, and sales tactics
- After enough social proof, people DM you without any CTA
- Max has had $20K contracts close entirely in DMs

---

## Authority Validation Loop

### Components

- Podcast appearances (Patrick Bet-David, etc.)
- Event attendance (Fan Bases, industry events)
- Client co-appearances (photos with successful clients)
- Each reinforces the loop

### Subconscious Authority Building

- People see you at prestigious events = elevated status
- People see you with successful clients = bridge perception
- Growth narrative embedded in your grid (old posts to new)

---

## Content for Conversion (Not Vanity)

### What Matters

- Who you've helped (not follower count or likes)
- Transformation stories with specific before/after positions
- Case study titles that paint a picture

### Case Study Framing (Max's Examples)

- "Coach with 200 followers generating 30K/month in 45 days"
- "Consultant secured 15K engagements bypassing high follower counts"
- "Service provider with under 1,000 followers outsold 100K+ follower competitors"
- Focus on relatable starting points and specific outcomes

---

## CTA Strategy

### Frequency

- Once per week maximum
- Constant CTAs = desperate, not authoritative

### Best CTA Type

- Testimonial video of someone in your ICP's position who succeeded with you
- Long-form (20 minute testimonial video)
- Shows transformation: where they were, you as the bridge, where they are now

### What NOT to Do

- Don't constantly say "buy this, join this, DM me, swipe up"
- Don't be too available (perceived as low value)
- Don't make every post a pitch

---

## Content Sequencing

- Grid tells a compelling narrative
- Transformation story embedded in post sequence
- Mix formats: carousels, insights, testimonials, authority photos
- Consistent branding throughout

---

## Objection Elimination Architecture

- Show every possible ICP problem solved with your clients
- Talk about client transformation from problem to solution
- Creates the "inevitability bridge" -- people want to work with you without knowing why

---

## Action Items

1. Audit your current content. Is it engineered for conversion or vanity? If you're chasing likes and followers instead of showing client results, restructure.
2. Build your social proof library: collect every testimonial, screenshot, result, and transformation story you have. Put them in highlights (50-100 per highlight).
3. Plan content around the Selective Exposure Funnel: strategic exposure of social proof over time, not random posting.
4. Limit CTAs to once per week max. When you do post a CTA, make it a testimonial video.
5. Create at least 3 case study posts using Max's framing: relatable starting point + specific outcome (e.g., "Coach with 200 followers generating 30K/month in 45 days").
6. Build the authority validation loop: get on podcasts, attend events, take photos with clients and successful people. Post these strategically across your grid.
7. Check your content for the inevitability bridge: can a prospect scroll your profile and see every version of their problem solved? If not, fill the gaps.
