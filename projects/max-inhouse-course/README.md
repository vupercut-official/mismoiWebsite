# Max Inhouse - Info Business Scaling Course

A study course built from Max Inhouse's complete info business scaling system. Max is a small town kid from Ohio who got banned on Meta at age 16 while making 3x his teacher's income. He scaled to 10K days, has clients hitting 15K days, is #1 ranked business on blog, appeared on Patrick Bet-David's podcast, and generated $183,485 for info clients in 60 days. His company: Stealth Scaling / Aurelian Circle.

This is not a beginner guide or a "how to go viral" course. It's a scaling solution for existing info businesses -- coaches, consultants, and agency owners targeting $30K-$100K/month.

## The Promise

A complete system from identity to fulfillment that covers: how to build authority online, find and qualify leads, run DM outreach, deliver value through Looms, book and close calls, track performance, create content that converts, and build a team to scale.

## Module List (Max's Exact Order)

1. **Digital Identity** - Your online identity IS your business. You will hit NONE of your KPIs without a good digital identity. This comes first.
2. **Lead Generation** - ICP qualification, lead sources, targeting criteria
3. **DM Outreach** - Instagram DM strategy, pattern interrupts, qualification framework
4. **Loom Strategy** - Bridging DMs to calls through free value
5. **Booking Calls** - Never say "call," reframing, objection handling, show-up optimization
6. **KPIs and Tracking** - 6 core metrics, daily targets, optimization triggers
7. **Content Strategy** - Selective exposure funnel, social proof loops, authority validation
8. **Sales Calls** - Max's word-for-word opening frame, call philosophy, closing
9. **Fulfillment and Team** - Delivery loop, early client approach, setter training, systems

## How to Use This Course

- Go through the modules in order. Max structured it this way for a reason.
- Identity is Module 1 because everything downstream depends on it. Your response rates, loom acceptance, booking rates, close rates -- all of it ties back to how your profile looks.
- Do the action items at the end of each module before moving on.
- Use Max's exact scripts and templates. They're battle-tested.
- Track your KPIs from day one so you know what to fix.
