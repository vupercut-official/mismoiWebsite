No matter how good the in-email observation is, "interesting email" to "monthly retainer" requires trust that a single cold email can't build. Vu has no case studies. The prospect has no reference point. That jump doesn't happen.

The fix isn't more offers. It's a $97-197 pilot step between the audit and the retainer. "I'll build your first AI follow-up sequence in 48 hours, no contract." Low enough to be a no-brainer. High enough to filter browsers from buyers. And it gives Vu an actual result to show before asking for $500/mo.

On the contrarian's Dead Lead Reactivation Sequence: this is underrated in this discussion. Every other offer creates awareness of a problem. The reactivation sequence is the only one that gives them something they can deploy today. That changes the psychology entirely. Observations create discomfort. Tools create dependency. Dependency converts.

That's the offer I'd add if I could swap one out: not an audit, not a report, but a working 3-email AI follow-up sequence written for their market tier. Vu time: 30 min with a template. Prospect time: zero. CTA: "Want me to send the sequence?"

Revised priority from a conversion path standpoint:
1. Speed test (problem awareness, in-email)
2. Profile observation (problem specificity, in-email)
3. Reactivation sequence (immediate tool, zero friction)
4. Bridge ask: $97 pilot build
5. Retainer conversation

The 6 offers aren't 6 parallel options. They're stages in a trust ladder. If the framework doesn't acknowledge that, the campaign produces goodwill and no revenue.
