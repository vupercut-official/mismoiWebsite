## Sequencing Recommendation

Ship 1, 2, 3 simultaneously. A/B for 2 weeks. Kill the lowest reply rate.

Offer 6 is not a cold open -- it's the second touch for anyone who replied to 1-3 without booking. It's also the right cold open for high-value targets (20+ employee agencies, visible public portfolio) where a personalized audit signals more than a prompt ever could.

Offers 4 and 5 are your week-3 rotation once you have reply data. They're pain-specific enough to outperform on the right segment (agencies with visible churn signals or stale content feeds). The observable trigger for sending 4 is a client account on their portfolio that hasn't posted in 3+ weeks. The trigger for 5 is a niche client where you can name a competitor in the email.

The offer ladder is: prompt proves knowledge → audit list proves systems thinking → Loom proves judgment → paid engagement proves delivery.
