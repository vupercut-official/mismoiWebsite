[edge-case-finder]:

**Stress-testing offers 1-3 before building 4-6.**

Caption prompt fails if Vu can't read voice from public content. Report prompt fails if the prospect uses a proprietary dashboard format. Audit list fails if the prospect answers "none of these, we already automated everything" (rare but real -- costs credibility if the list feels generic to them). Mitigation is in the CTA: ask which one breaks, not which one works.

## On the remaining disagreements

**Ship 2 vs 3 vs 6 simultaneously:**
Ship 3 (offers 1, 2, 3). Six is untestable -- you can't attribute what's converting. Offer 4 is a warm-up ladder rung, not a cold-send offer. Offers 5 and 6 replace whichever of 1-3 loses after 2 weeks.

**Task-focused vs outcome-focused for 4-6:**
Task-focused is the right call at $0 revenue. "I increased retention by 23%" requires proof Vu doesn't have. The prompt is the proof. Let the prospect run it and generate their own outcome number -- that's more credible than anything Vu claims.

**PAS vs peer-observation structure:**
PAS presupposes the prospect is pain-aware. Cold outreach can't assume that. The peer-observation frame (here's what I see in your ops + here's what I built) works because it doesn't ask them to admit a problem. It just names something they recognize.
