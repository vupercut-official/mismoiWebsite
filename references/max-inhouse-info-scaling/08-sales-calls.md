# Sales Call Structure

## Max's Opening Frame (Word for Word)

"Listen, I'm not a sales person. I've never learned traditional sales. I don't know sales tactics. All I am is a very good business consultant. I'm going to hop on a call with you. You're going to tell me why you took an hour out of your day to hop on a call with me instead of someone else. You're going to walk me through your bottlenecks. I'm going to tell you how I would fix them. You're going to realize I'm really good at what I do. And then you're going to want me to pitch you."

### Why This Works
- Disarms the sales guard completely
- Positions as consultant, not salesperson
- Makes them explain their own motivation (commitment device)
- Frames the pitch as something THEY will ask for
- Honest and direct -- people appreciate directness
- Not scripted -- came naturally and stuck because it's authentic

## Call Structure Philosophy

- Be upfront and direct
- Don't waste time
- Be a consultant, not a closer
- Give genuine value on the call
- Let them realize your competence through demonstration
- The pitch should feel like a natural next step, not a hard close

## One-Call Close vs Two-Call Close

- Max does one-call closes now
- Two-call close is valid for beginners
- The DM + Loom process pre-sells, so by the time they're on the call, trust is already established

## Key Principles

- Your appointment setter should understand this entire system
- Train setters extensively on conversation frameworks
- Max spent a year training with his setter
- The pre-call nurturing (DMs, Loom, social proof) does most of the selling

## When Close Rate is Low

- Call structure needs to change
- Offer needs to become more aligned with prospect's values
- Re-examine the pre-call nurturing process
- Are you attracting the right ICP?
