# KPIs and Performance Tracking

## Core Metrics

### 1. Initial Response Rate
- **Target:** 40%+
- **How to hit it:** DM engagers, swipe up on stories of people who've engaged with your profile
- **Critical dependency:** You will NOT hit these KPIs without a good digital identity
- Story replies: 70-80% response rate
- Cold DMs: 30-40% with authority, 5-10% without

### 2. Conversation Rate
- **Target:** 70-80% of people replying continue the conversation
- **If below target:** Start sending voice messages
- Voice messages increase perceived authenticity
- People won't think it's BS when they hear your voice

### 3. Loom Acceptance Rate
- **Target:** 30-40% of conversations accept the Loom
- If you can get 100%, do it
- Key driver: pain point identification
- If you don't know their pain point, you can't position the right Loom

### 4. Call Booking Rate
- **Target:** 50-60% of Loom viewers book a call
- Key drivers: urgency (genuine), proper positioning
- Use reframing language (never say "call")

### 5. Show-Up Rate
- **Target:** 80%+ of bookings show up
- Max hits ~92% due to authority + nurturement
- Key drivers: confirmation flow, day-before/day-of reminders, voice messages

### 6. Close Rate
- **Target:** 20-30% of show-ups close
- 50% = godly
- If below target: call structure needs to change, offer needs better alignment with values

## Daily Activity Targets

| Activity | Target | Time Estimate |
|---|---|---|
| New outreach (cold DMs) | 20-30 profiles | < 30 minutes |
| Follow-ups (best conversations) | 15-20 | ~30 min with voice messages |
| Loom sends | 5-10 | Varies |
| Call bookings | 3-5 per day | Result of above |

## Pipeline Math (Working Backwards)

If you want 3 closes per week at 25% close rate:
- Need 12 show-ups/week
- At 80% show rate = 15 bookings/week
- At 55% booking rate = ~27 Loom views/week
- At 35% Loom acceptance = ~77 conversations/week
- At 75% conversation rate = ~103 initial replies/week
- At 40% response rate = ~258 DMs/week (~37/day)

## Optimization Triggers

### Low Response Rate (< 30%)
- Fix: Profile/identity work, better targeting
- Check: Are you DMing engagers or cold prospects?

### Low Conversation Rate (< 60%)
- Fix: Better openers, voice messages
- Check: Are your messages pattern interrupts or generic?

### Low Loom Acceptance (< 25%)
- Fix: Better pain point discovery
- Check: Are you identifying their specific problem before offering the Loom?

### Low Booking Rate (< 40%)
- Fix: Loom content, CTA language
- Check: Is your Loom showing tangible value? Are you using "call" language?

### Low Show Rate (< 70%)
- Fix: Confirmation flow, reminders
- Check: Voice message confirmations? Day-before + day-of reminders?

### Low Close Rate (< 15%)
- Fix: Call structure, offer alignment
- Check: Is your offer solving what they actually need?

## Tracking Infrastructure

- Log every DM sent, reply received, Loom sent, call booked, show/no-show, close/no-close
- Without tracking data, improvement is impossible
- Your setter should know this system inside out
