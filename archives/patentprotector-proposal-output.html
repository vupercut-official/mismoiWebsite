1. Market Research & Intelligence

                        
Apify scrapes competitor content, Reddit, Twitter, and Instagram for patent-related pain points and angles. This identifies trending topics and language your ICP actually uses. Feeds research directly into the intelligence hub — no manual input needed. Updates weekly to stay current.

                        
Aggregates all performance data from YouTube, Instagram, TikTok. Signal aggregator identifies high-performing outliers automatically and weeds out factors that caused underperforming content. Stores ICP language, winning angles, and client feedback data.

                        

                            
                        

                    

                


                
                

                    

                        
2. AI Video Generation Pipeline

                        
Claude scripts 1-2 videos per day (30 sec - 1 min) based on intelligence hub data. Google Veo generates the video from scripts — fully AI, no recording needed. Each batch of videos is better than the last because scripts are informed by real performance data.

                        
Content style: testimonial-style, product demos, "how I filed a patent in minutes" angles.

                        

                            
                        

                    

                


                
                

                    

                        
3. Multi-Platform Auto-Poster & Optimize

                        
Posts automatically to YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels, TikTok. Schedules for optimal posting times per platform. Performance data feeds back into the signal aggregator.

                        
The flywheel: research → script → generate → post → measure → improve → repeat.
