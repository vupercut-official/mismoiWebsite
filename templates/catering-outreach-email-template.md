# Catering Outreach Email Template -- Banh Mi Ong Beo

**Client:** Banh Mi Ong Beo (Chantilly & Fairfax, VA)
**Purpose:** Cold outreach to local businesses for recurring catering orders
**Framework:** PAS (Pain-Agitate-Solve)
**Rules:** Plain text, under 75 words, one soft CTA, no links, no HTML

---

## Subject Line Options (A/B test these)

1. `catering for {{business_name}}`
2. `lunch for your team`
3. `your next event menu`
4. `catering question`

## Usage Instructions

### How to fill in a template:

1. **Pick the business type** from the variable bank above
2. **Find one signal** about the specific business (their website, Google, Instagram, event calendar)
3. **Write the signal observation** in one sentence -- reference something real and specific
4. **Plug in the pain hook and value prop** from their business type section
5. **Choose the CTA** from their section (or use the default)
6. **Pick nearest location** -- Chantilly (13973 Metrotech Dr) or Fairfax (10631 Braddock Rd)
7. **Send all 4 emails** on the 1-7-14-21 day cadence

### Rules (from research):

- Under 75 words per email body
- Plain text only -- no images, no links, no HTML
- No spam words (free, guaranteed, act now, limited time)
- One question max per email
- Lowercase subject lines
- Send from a secondary domain (not the restaurant's primary domain)
- Max 30-50 emails per day per mailbox
- Verify all email addresses before sending

### Tracking:

- Log each send in the Google Sheet from the lead scraper system
- Track: sent date, business type, which email in sequence, reply Y/N
- After 4-6 weeks of data, you'll know which business types convert best -- double down on those
